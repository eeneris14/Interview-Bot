Simple Interview Tips

  • Research the organization you are applying to.
  • Visualize and rehearse in advance.
  • Prepare for behavioral questions that put you in a hypothetical scenario.
  • Take care of your appearance by exuding confidence and balancing the use of cologne/perfume.
  • Be conscious of your body language, making sure not to exhibit agitated movements.
  • Be a good listener.
  • Ask questions after the interview to take advantage of showcasing your communication skills.
  • Pause and think, then talk. Don’t feel compelled to answer the questions immediately.
  • Make follow-ups and leave thank-you notes, even if it’s a rejection.
  • Remember, preparation is key!
